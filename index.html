Projects

Super Space Trip

Super Space Trip is a mobile 2D platformer game about a robot who has lost control of his spaceship and crash-landed on a strange planet.

Players must help the robot survive and collect the resources that he needs to fix the ship and escape.

The game is being developed by me in Unity using C# scripts and features a variety of challenging levels, enemies, and obstacles.

Orange Reef Adventure

Orange Reef Adventure is a mobile 2D arcade game with a focus on survival and scoring points. Players control an orange, fluffy creature in the ocean as it fights to survive for as long as possible.

It is being made all by myself in Godot using GDScript and is almost finished.

Restaurante

The Restaurante is a C++ project that I undertook with another student to apply the skills We developed during our studies at ATEC.

The project involved the creation of a restaurant management system, encompassing order management, customer management, employee management, and inventory management.

AeroMills

AeroMills is a C++ project made by me and two colleagues at ATEC. It helps on renting airplanes.

The project contains features for managing bookings, customer information, and rental history. All data is saved in simple CSV files for easy access.
